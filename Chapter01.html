Homemade Pizza Recipe

This homemade pizza recipe is easy to make and delicious. Follow these simple steps to create a perfect pizza at home.

Ingredients

• 2 cups all-purpose flour

• 1 packet (7g) dry yeast

• ¾ cup warm water

• 1 tbsp olive oil

• ½ tsp salt

• ½ cup pizza sauce

• 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

• ½ cup toppings (pepperoni, mushrooms, olives, etc.)

Method

1. In a bowl, mix warm water and yeast. Let it sit for 5 minutes.

2. Add flour, salt, and olive oil. Mix well to form a dough.

3. Knead the dough for 5-7 minutes until smooth.

4. Cover and let it rise for 1 hour.

5. Preheat the oven to 220°C (425°F).

6. Roll out the dough on a floured surface.

7. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the dough.

8. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese on top.

9. Add your favorite toppings.

10. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until golden brown.

11. Remove from oven, slice, and enjoy!
