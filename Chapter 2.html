Pac-Man

Pac-Man is a legendary arcade game developed by Namco and released in 1980. It quickly became one of the most iconic and influential video games of all time, known for its simple yet addictive gameplay. Players control Pac-Man as he navigates a maze, eating pellets while avoiding ghosts.

Key Information

Developer: Namco

Release Date: May 22, 1980

Genre: Arcade, Maze

Platforms: Arcade, Atari, PlayStation, PC, Mobile, and more

Characters

Pac-Man: The yellow, circular hero who eats pellets to clear the maze.

Blinky: The red ghost, also known as Shadow, who aggressively chases Pac-Man.

Pinky: The pink ghost, known for ambushing Pac-Man.

Inky: The blue ghost, unpredictable and tricky.

Clyde: The orange ghost, who sometimes follows Pac-Man but can also wander.
